Program Notes

The Summer Research Internship in Biomedical Sciences offers undergraduate students a unique opportunity to engage in cutting-edge research at one of the world's leading medical institutions. Participants will work alongside renowned faculty and graduate students on projects spanning molecular biology, genetics, neuroscience, and immunology.

Interns will gain hands-on laboratory experience, attend weekly seminars featuring distinguished speakers, participate in professional development workshops, and present their research findings at a culminating symposium. This program is designed to prepare students for graduate school and careers in biomedical research.

The program includes mentorship from faculty advisors, networking opportunities with peers and professionals, and access to Stanford's state-of-the-art research facilities. Housing is provided in campus residences, and participants receive a competitive stipend plus meal allowance.
